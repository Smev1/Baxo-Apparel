ABOUT BAXO

Founded in 2020, Baxo is a contemporary clothing brand that focuses on minimalist design, premium materials, and sustainable production methods. Our pieces are designed to be timeless, versatile, and of the highest quality.

We believe that fashion should be both beautiful and responsible. That's why we work with ethical manufacturers and use eco-friendly materials whenever possible. Each Baxo piece is crafted to last, reducing waste and environmental impact.
